  • About

    • I'm a Vancouver-based designer & illustrator.

  • I'm a freelance designer and a Branding student at Capilano University in North Vancouver studying Design and Visual Communication. I am an introspective creative whose explorative work is fuelled by nature, photography, music, fashion and beauty with sustainability at the core. I try to find beauty within the subtleties of life by striving to transform the mundane. Creating meaningful human connections is the driving force of my work being mindful of different perspectives. I love finding new ways to conceptualize ideas into stories.
